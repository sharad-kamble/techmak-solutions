Manufacturing Capabilities

End-to-end forging, heat treatment, and inspection capabilities delivering strength, precision, and reliability.

Forging Capacity

We offer a wide range of forging solutions designed to meet demanding industrial applications with high strength and dimensional accuracy.

  • Closed Die Forgings ranging from 1 kg to 50 kg
  • Open Die Forgings ranging from 1 kg to 1000 kg
  • Ring Rolling capability with diameters from 200 mm to 3000 mm

Heat Treatment

Our in-house heat treatment processes enhance mechanical properties, ensuring optimal performance, durability, and metallurgical consistency.

  • Normalizing for uniform grain structure
  • Hardening & Tempering for improved strength and toughness
  • Annealing for stress relief and machinability
  • Solution Annealing for corrosion-resistant alloys

Testing & Inspection

Stringent quality control and inspection procedures ensure compliance with international standards and customer specifications.

  • 100% Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI)
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT) for internal defect detection
  • Hardness testing for material verification
  • Dimensional inspection for precision assurance
  • Third-party inspection available on request
