About TechMak

Engineering Excellence in Forging & Machining Solutions

Established in 2019, TechMak is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, committed to delivering high-quality forged and machined components that meet global standards.

Based in Pune, Maharashtra (India), we specialize in Global Supply Chain Management, offering reliable and cost-effective manufacturing solutions to industries across India and the United States.

With strong technical expertise and a customer-first approach, we supply forged and machined parts through Closed Die Forging, Open Die Forging, and Ring Rolled Forging routes, ensuring precision, durability, and consistency in every product.

Quality Assurance

Quality is the backbone of Tech Mak Solutions. Every component undergoes comprehensive testing and inspection, ensuring compliance with national and international standards.

    Non-destructive testing to ensure internal integrity.

  • Mechanical & Chemical Testing

    Ensures all components meet mechanical and chemical standards.

  • Dimensional Inspection

    Precision measurement for exact specifications.

  • Heat Treatment

    Performed as per specification and standards.

  • Surface, Hardness & Microstructure

  • Material Traceability

    Full documentation from raw material to finished product.
